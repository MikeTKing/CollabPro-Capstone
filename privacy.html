Privacy Policy

At CollabPro, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information when you use our application.

Data Collection

We collect the following information when you use CollabPro:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and password provided during signup.
  • Task Data: Task titles, descriptions, priorities, due dates, assignees, and statuses created or edited within the app.
  • Collaboration Data: Comments and notifications generated during task collaboration.

Data Storage

All data collected by CollabPro is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Your data is only accessible on the device and browser you use to access the application.
  • Data persists until you clear your browser's storage or manually reset the application.

Data Security

Since CollabPro is a client-side application:

  • We do not encrypt data stored in localStorage. This means your data could be accessed by malicious scripts if your device is compromised.
  • We recommend using the application on a secure device and browser to minimize risks.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties, as all data remains on your device.

Your Rights

You can manage your data by:

  • Editing or deleting your profile information within the app.
  • Clearing your browser's localStorage to remove all app data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the application evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
